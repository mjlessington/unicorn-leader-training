# The Daring Unicorn: Cultivating Courageous and Exceptional Leadership in IT

## Combining *Be The Unicorn* by William Vanderbloemen & *Dare To Lead* by Brene Brown

---

## Why This Training Exists

The technology sector faces a persistent leadership paradox: organizations invest heavily in technical talent development while underinvesting in the human skills that determine whether teams thrive or fracture. The result is a landscape filled with technically brilliant leaders who struggle to build trust, navigate conflict, and inspire the people around them.

This training bridges that gap by weaving together two powerful leadership frameworks:

- **Be The Unicorn** (William Vanderbloemen) — Identifies the 12 habits that make certain professionals irreplaceable, drawing from Vanderbloemen's experience evaluating over 30,000 leaders. These habits are learnable, measurable, and directly applicable to IT leadership.

- **Dare To Lead** (Brene Brown) — Defines courageous leadership through the lens of vulnerability, values, trust, and resilience. Brown's research-backed framework gives leaders the tools to show up authentically in high-stakes environments.

Together, these frameworks create a complete model: **Vanderbloemen tells you what unicorn leaders *do*; Brown tells you what unicorn leaders *feel* and how they build the relational infrastructure to sustain high performance.**

## Who This Training Is For

- **IT Senior Leaders** looking to evolve from operational management to transformational leadership
- **IT Directors and Senior Managers** building and scaling high-performing teams
- **Aspiring IT Leaders** preparing for their first leadership role
- **HR and L&D Partners** supporting IT leadership development programs

## How to Use This Training

### Self-Paced Individual Study
Work through each module sequentially. Complete the reflection exercises and self-assessments. Use the 90-day action plan in Module 6 to apply what you learn.

### Facilitated Group Workshop
Use the [Facilitator Guide](/training/resources/facilitator-guide) to run interactive sessions. Each module includes discussion prompts, group exercises, and breakout activities designed for cohorts of 8-20 participants.

### Leadership Team Retreat
Compress the training into an intensive 2-day retreat format. Focus on the exercises and group discussions, assigning the reading content as pre-work.
