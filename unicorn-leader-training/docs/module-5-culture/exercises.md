## Exercise 4: Culture Scaling Design (20 minutes)

### For Groups

Design a 90-day culture scaling plan for your IT organization. Choose one cultural practice you want to spread:

**The practice:** _______________

**Phase 1: Model (Days 1-30)**
- How will you and your team demonstrate this practice visibly?
- What results will you document?
- Who are your early allies?

**Phase 2: Invite (Days 31-60)**
- How will you share the practice with peer teams without being prescriptive?
- What forums or formats will you use?
- How will you make it easy for others to try?

**Phase 3: Embed (Days 61-90)**
- What organizational system will you propose changing to support this practice?
- Who needs to approve or champion the systemic change?
- How will you measure adoption?

## Group Discussion Questions

1. **"Culture is what happens when the leader isn't in the room."** What happens on your team when you're not there? Does the culture hold?

2. **What's the most toxic cultural pattern you've experienced in an IT organization?** How was it maintained? What would have been needed to change it?

3. **"You can't build a culture of trust in an organization that tolerates brilliant jerks."** Is this absolutely true, or are there nuances?

4. **Think about an IT leader you've worked with who practiced servant leadership.** What was different about their team compared to others? What was their career trajectory?
