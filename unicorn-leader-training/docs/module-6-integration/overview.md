# Module 6: Integration & Action Planning

**Duration:** 90 minutes | **Format:** Individual Work + Peer Sharing + Commitment Ceremony

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. Synthesize their learning from all five modules into a personal leadership manifesto
2. Create a concrete 90-day action plan for applying both frameworks
3. Establish a peer accountability structure that sustains growth beyond the training
4. Articulate their leadership identity as a unicorn who dares to lead

## The Integration Challenge

You've covered a lot of ground:

| Module | Core Concepts |
|--------|--------------|
| **Module 1** | 12 unicorn traits, 4 courage skill sets, the IT leadership gap |
| **Module 2** | Self-awareness, authenticity, values identification, emotional intelligence |
| **Module 3** | Hiring, psychological safety, BRAVING trust, team building |
| **Module 4** | Brave conversations, change leadership, innovation and failure |
| **Module 5** | Values-driven culture, servant leadership, scaling culture |

The risk at this point is information overload — absorbing everything conceptually but applying nothing practically. This module exists to prevent that.

Brown warns: *"Knowledge without practice is philosophy. Practice without knowledge is experiment. The combination of knowledge and deliberate practice is transformation."*

## The Three Outputs of This Module

By the end of this session, you will have:

1. **A Personal Leadership Manifesto** — A one-page document that captures who you are as a leader, what you stand for, and how you will show up
2. **A 90-Day Action Plan** — Three specific, measurable commitments with accountability structures
3. **A Peer Accountability Partnership** — A named partner who will check in with you monthly

These are not theoretical exercises. They are the bridge between this training room and your Monday morning.
